Hi, my name is Matt. I work as a programmer, and sometimes draw in my free time. I'm mainly using this space as a way to document my drawings and animations.

Website

The site functions as a personal wiki, where I collect information that I have learned, experienced, or otherwise find interesting. It is a living document that grows and changes overtime. It is heavily inspired by 100 rabbits.

The site loads no javascript. It does not use cookies or third-party analytics.

The site is made up of static HTML content generated by a C binary. It can be viewed offline without the need for other tools or local servers.
